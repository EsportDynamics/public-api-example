Integration Flow

1. Configure Set RANKACY_BASE_URL and RANKACY_TOKEN in your env.
2. Upload Send a .dem file via POST /demos/upload.
3. Poll Watch demo status until SUCCESS or FAILED.
4. Inspect Retrieve kills and players to decide what to render.
5. Create Queue highlights automatically, by ticks, or by kill ID.
6. Monitor Track highlight status and receive webhook callbacks.
